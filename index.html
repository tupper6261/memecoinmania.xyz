Memecoin Mania

A match-3 game featuring some of today's most popular memecoins and released as an open edition NFT.

A Web3 Experiment

The goal of Memecoin Mania is to create some fun and utility within the memecoin community while adding scarcity to the featured tokens. The amount of scarcity is determined by the token owners, adding an element of friendly competition between communities.

Mechanics

  • Memecoin Mania can only be purchased with the memecoins featured in the game (listed below). You can spend however many of your token of choice (with a minimum of 2).
  • Half of your purchase cost gets burned at time of purchase.
  • With the remaining half of the purchase tokens:
    • We'll keep a running total of the USD value of how much of each token has been sent to the contract.
    • Whichever token's total purchasing value is the highest at the end of the sale - all those tokens get burned.
  • With the remaining tokens:
    • 10% goes to charity
    • 10% goes to the wallet who spent the most USD value on acquiring Memecoin Mania NFTs.
    • 80% goes to project costs
